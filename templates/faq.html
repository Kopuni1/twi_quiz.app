Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Twi Learning Platform?

It is an interactive platform designed to help you learn, practice, and improve your Twi language skills through lessons, games, word-of-the-day features, and pronunciation tools.

2. Do I need an account to use the platform?

Some features like the Word of the Day and quiz tracking require an account. However, basic browsing and learning resources may be available without logging in.

3. Is the platform free?

Yes! Most features are completely free. Additional premium or advanced features may be introduced in the future, but core learning tools remain accessible to everyone.

4. How often is new content added?

New words, quizzes, and features are added regularly to keep your learning experience fresh and engaging.

5. How can I report mistakes or suggest new features?

You can use our Contact Page to send feedback, suggestions, or corrections. We appreciate your input!
